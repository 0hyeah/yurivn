Help Index > Sell Item
Go to My Items and enter decided price in Sell Item field. Then click Do Selected Actions


If you change your mind, you can apply new price by enter new value in Apply New Price field, click Do Selected Actions


You items will be available in kShop


If you don't want to sell item anymore, just check the box labeled Stop Selling and click Do Selected Actions or click Stop Selling. Item will no longer in kShop


Note: You can also sell an item by click Sell Item. You can change item description and specify buyer by that way
