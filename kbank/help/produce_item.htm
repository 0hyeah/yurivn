Help Index > Produce Item
Click Factory from kBank popup menu


Click Produce of itemtype you want to produce


In the coming form, you can choose name (can not be changed later), description,.v.v. You can also specify buyer for item. In Active Duration select box, you must choose one, item price is base on this


After pressing Produce, item will be available immediately on kShop


You can check your history by click View All History from kBank popup menu


History show that you have produce item with total amount of 40000
