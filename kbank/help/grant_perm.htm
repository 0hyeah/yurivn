Help Index > Grant Permission
Click Account Management from kBank popup menu. You can see Grant Permission section like this. In the first line of Granted Permission it's you with your permissions


And here is Grant Permission section of a Company


In some case, company1 want to give user1 permission to access his account (including money, items). company1 must fill user1 userid OR username into Allowed Permission section, then click Do Selected Actions


Done! user1 has been granted permission successfully!


Check Granted Permission section of user1:


From now on, user1 can select account to access (donate, buy items, place bid,.v.v.)


If company1 don't want user1 has permission anymore, just check the box labeled Remove and click Do Selected Actions
