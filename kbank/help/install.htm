Help Index > Install kBank
First of all, thank you for using my product. Any question or suggestion please contact me at mrpaint@gmail.com. And now, let's install kBank to your vBulletin board!

Step 1: Install and Upload (I believe you have done this step correctly)
Go to your AdminCP, click Manage Products under Plugins & Products, click [Add/Import Product]


Browse to "product-kbank.xml" and click Import


Wait for a moment and you will get the this message


Now, you have to use a FTP client to upload all files/folders in upload folder to your forum root


Step 2: Config
After step 1, you will get this in the right panel of your AdminCP


The most important thing must be edit immediately is AdminID(s) in kBank Settings. Click Edit Settings under kBank to access kBank Settings. Look for AdminID(s) and type userid of the member you want to be kBank Administrator


Now, you have to add some usergroup to use as Companies group and Bank Rupted Users group. You can choose existing usergroup or add new usergroup (click here for detail instruction) then go to kBank Settings and add new value


Another important part is Monthly Tax. To begin you should post a new thread for members who can't pay tax, something like this:


Then you go to kBank Settings and add some values for Monthly Tax


The value for Monthly Tax - Step is different from default value. I think it's better. With this new formular, Monthly Tax will be apply to the total value of income (posting, receive from companies, receiver from kBank). There are some example for you to understand:
  • Member A: income = 350. A will be applied Monthly Tax at 2% (350 < 500). Tax = 350x2% = 7. Very simple!
  • Member B: income = 800. It's little bit complicated! B will be applied 2% with the first 500 and 5% with the rest (300). Tax = 500x2% + 300x5% = 10 + 15 = 25
  • Member C: income = 3000. Wow! That is a lot of money! Tax = 500x2% + 500x5% + 1000x10% + 1000x25% = 10 + 25 + 100 + 250 = 385
